Calculation Methodology

Daily Reducing with Month-End Posting

Interest is accrued daily on outstanding principal. At month-end, total accrued interest is posted as a debit (added to outstanding). EMIs and prepayments reduce principal immediately.

Daily Interest = Outstanding × Annual Rate ÷ 365

Part Period Interest

For days between loan start date and the first month-end, interest is charged separately as "Part Period Interest" (matches actual bank behavior).

EMI Formula

EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1)
